Chocolate Chip Cookies

classic chocolate chocolate chip cookies fresh out of the oven

Classic chocolate chip cookies are a timeless dessert consisting of a soft and chewy cookie base made of flour, sugar, eggs, and vanilla, studded with chunks of chocolate chips. They are a staple in bakeries and homes and are loved for their comforting and satisfying taste.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2C all-purpose flours
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1C unsalted butter, room temperature
  • 3/4C white sugar
  • 3/4C brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chip cookies

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In a large bowl, cream together the butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until light and fluffy.
  4. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla extract.
  5. Gradually stir in the flour mixture until just combined.
  6. Fold in the chocolate chips.
  7. Drop tablespoon-sized balls of dough onto the prepared baking sheet.
  8. Bake for 9 to 11 minutes, or until lightly golden brown.
  9. Remove from the oven and let cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.
